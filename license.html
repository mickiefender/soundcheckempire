License Information

Understanding your rights when purchasing beats

When you purchase a beat from Kojo Nytro, you are purchasing a license to use the beat according to specific terms. This page explains the different license types available and what rights each license grants you.

All licenses are non-transferable and grant you the right to use the beat in your projects according to the specific terms of your license. The copyright to the composition and master recording remains with Kojo Nytro unless you purchase an exclusive license.

License Types

Basic License ($49.99)

The Basic License is perfect for independent artists looking to create and distribute music on a smaller scale. This license grants you:

  • Non-exclusive rights to use the beat in one commercial recording
  • Distribution of up to 5,000 copies (streams, downloads, physical copies combined)
  • Use in one music video
  • Monetization on streaming platforms (YouTube, SoundCloud, etc.)
  • Credit must be given as "Produced by Kojo Nytro"

Premium License ($99.99)

The Premium License is designed for growing artists with a larger audience. This license includes all Basic License rights, plus:

  • Distribution of up to 50,000 copies (streams, downloads, physical copies combined)
  • Use in unlimited music videos
  • Radio broadcasting rights (non-commercial stations)
  • Use in podcasts and non-profit performances
  • Credit must be given as "Produced by Kojo Nytro"

Unlimited License ($199.99)

The Unlimited License is ideal for established artists looking for maximum flexibility. This license includes all Premium License rights, plus:

  • Unlimited distribution
  • Commercial radio broadcasting rights
  • Use in commercial performances and tours
  • Use in multiple projects
  • Credit must be given as "Produced by Kojo Nytro"

Exclusive License (Contact for pricing)

The Exclusive License transfers ownership rights of the beat to you. Once purchased, the beat will be removed from our catalog and will not be sold to anyone else. This license grants you:

  • Full ownership of the master recording (the beat)
  • Unlimited distribution and use in any project
  • All rights to monetize and commercialize the beat
  • Trackout files (individual instrument stems)
  • Credit is appreciated but not required

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a non-exclusive license?

A non-exclusive license means that the beat can be sold to multiple customers. You have the right to use the beat according to your license terms, but other artists may also purchase and use the same beat.

What is an exclusive license?

An exclusive license means that once purchased, the beat becomes exclusively yours. We will remove it from our catalog, and no one else will be able to purchase it. You will own the rights to the master recording.

Do I need to give credit?

Yes, all non-exclusive licenses require you to credit Kojo Nytro as the producer. The standard credit format is "Produced by Kojo Nytro" in your track description, metadata, and any other relevant locations.

Can I modify the beat?

Yes, you can add vocals, adjust the arrangement, and make other modifications to fit your artistic vision. However, you cannot claim the modified beat as your original production.

What are trackout files?

Trackout files are the individual instrument stems that make up the beat. These allow for more detailed mixing and customization of the beat. Trackout files are only included with Exclusive Licenses.
